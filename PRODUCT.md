# SimpleCRM — Product

## What it is
SimpleCRM is a lightweight CRM for a **solo operator** — one person running their own pipeline, contacts, and follow-ups single-handed. No teams, no permissions, no handoffs.

## The real job it does
Contacts and interaction logs are the foundation, but the **pipeline view is the moneymaker**: it surfaces *who is about to go cold* so the owner follows up before a warm lead slips. The core value is preventing revenue leakage from missed or late follow-ups — not merely storing records.

When the owner opens the app, they should **instantly see the top few contacts to follow up with today** — a "chase these now" list driven by deal stage and time-since-last-interaction.

## Users
- **Primary (and only) user:** a solo operator running their own sales process. Every surface optimizes for a single owner glancing in and instantly knowing who to chase today.

## Core capabilities (day-one MVP)
- **Pipeline view (hero feature)** — see deals by stage, with emphasis on surfacing at-risk / cold-going deals so the user can act in time. Opens to a "follow up today" shortlist.
- **Contact management** — add and manage the people/accounts being worked; feeds the pipeline.
- **Interaction logging** — record calls, emails, and meetings against each contact; the recency of interactions powers the cold-deal detection.

## Strategic principles
- Optimize for *follow-up before a lead goes cold*, not exhaustive data entry.
- Pipeline health (what needs attention now) is the primary surface, not a buried report.
- Built for one: no collaboration overhead — speed and clarity for a single owner.
- Keep it simple: fast capture, clear stages, low friction.

## Brand / tone
- Name: **SimpleCRM**.
- Logo: deep-blue minimal pipeline mark.
- Tone: clean, calm, focused. Gets out of the way so the owner can act fast.
